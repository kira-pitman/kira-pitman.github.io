Border, Margin, and Padding

Border

Border is the literal border around something on your page e.g. picture or text. can choose width, style, and colour of border, and came make curved border by using border-radius. The distance this sits from your item is controlled by the padding

Padding

Padding is the way we control the space between our content and it's borders. Can choose it's left, top, right, and bottom padding, as well as the colour.

Margin
